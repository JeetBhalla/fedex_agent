Recognition at Accenture

How we foster a culture of equality

At Accenture, we believe recognition and gratitude are essential for motivating individuals and fostering a positive work culture. It’s about more than acknowledging what someone does—it's about valuing who they are and showing appreciation. This approach helps create an environment where everyone feels valued, inspires people to bring their best selves to work, and encourages contributions that shine.​ ​

Ready to inspire greatness ?​

With the Recognize tool it’s easy to start (or to continue) sharing these virtual high-fives that make everyone feel like superstars. Best part, there are nearly 50 recognition awards available to help you show in-the-moment recognition, give gratitude, and celebrate performance milestones. So, think birthday, work anniversary, and life celebrations; acts of kindness; showcasing those demonstrating our Leadership Essentials; and much, much more. ​ You’ll be amazed by what happens next when you actively recognize the people around you. ​

Have your heard? It’s all about you and your impact, in one place.

Most recognition awards (excluding life events and anniversaries) are automatically captured in the Feedback Hub in Workday. That means you and your people lead have a comprehensive view across all your performance feedback and recognition. ​ So, when you’re acknowledged in the Recognize tool, your people lead can easily see the contributions that you’re making toward creating a positive culture in addition to reviewing your “anytime” performance feedback and priority reflections. It’s all about you and your impact, in one place. ​ Want to review your recognition in the Workday Feedback Hub? It’s simple. Just log into Workday and click on View Profile in the upper right corner under your profile picture. Then, in the left nav bar, click on Performance then click on More > Feedback received. You can also filter the recognition by type. Pretty cool, right? ​

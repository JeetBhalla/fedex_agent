What we do at @FedEx

FedEx is a 50-year-old Fortune 100 company headquartered in Memphis, Tennessee, United States. It provides a portfolio of transportation, e-commerce, and business services. FedEx is widely recognized for its innovation, using data from every journey to enhance tracking and visibility. It is also known for its strong commitment to sustainability and community support. It provides a portfolio of transportation, e-commerce, and business services. FedEx is widely recognized for its innovation, using data from every journey to enhance tracking and visibility. It is also known for its strong commitment to sustainability and community support. With a team of over 400,000 members, FedEx connects 99% of the world's GDP, showcasing its substantial impact on global trade and logistics. In its first 50 years of operation, FedEx's physical network of planes, trucks, and people created one of the world's most successful companies. As FedEx looks ahead to the next 50 years, it plans to leverage technology and data in innovative ways to "make supply chains smarter for everyone." To achieve this vision, FedEx has partnered with Accenture to modernize its technology and operations.

The collaboration with Accenture began with strategy and consulting for a new Chief Digital and Information Officer organization. It then progressed to a large Managed Services contract aimed at modernizing technology delivery and is now advancing into a journey-to-cloud program. This transformation focuses on the core of FedEx's business, enabling its transition from a logistics company to a technology-led organization.

The seven-year Managed Services contract with Accenture aims to enhance productivity and value delivery while reducing costs and accelerating delivery timelines. Accenture is providing services related to systems development, enhancement, support, and maintenance—capabilities that were previously spread across multiple vendors. Approximately 7,000 people are involved in delivering this initiative for FedEx, targeting a 33% cost saving.

This global partnership has delivery hubs in Memphis and Amsterdam, supported by delivery centers in India and the Philippines. The collaboration signifies a strategic step for FedEx as it continues to evolve and redefine its role in the logistics and technology industries.

Launchpad for New Joiner

    A comprehensive, seamless onboarding experience designed to ensure your smooth integration into the team.

  • Day 1 HRPA Connect: Start strong with an introduction to essential HR information and support.
  • People Lead Integration Sessions: Engage in meaningful sessions with your People Lead for clear direction and guidance.​
  • Cultural Sensitization Sessions: Immerse yourself in our culture and values to feel aligned from the outset.​
  • Query Resolution Connects: Have your questions answered promptly to ensure clarity and support.
  • Know the Client / Know Your Leaders: Gain valuable insights into the clients you'll be working with and our leadership team.
  • Did You Know Series: Stay informed with useful tips and important information throughout your journey.
  • New Joiner Surveys: Share your experiences and feedback to help us refine the onboarding process for future joiners.

    Key Benefits for You:

  • Build a Strong Network: Develop relationships with colleagues, leaders, and mentors across the organization, fostering a sense of community from day one.
  • Effective Alignment to Organizational Values: Deepen your understanding of our culture and core values, helping you navigate the company’s expectations and align your efforts with our mission and goals.​
  • Empowerment and Confidence in Your Role: Through targeted training and ongoing support, you’ll feel equipped and confident to perform at your best, ensuring you thrive in your new role.​
  • Positive Impact on Job Satisfaction and Retention: Engaging with our onboarding experience helps you feel valued and supported, resulting in higher job satisfaction and long-term career growth within the company.
  • Smooth Integration into Your New Role: With comprehensive resources, guidance, and clear expectations, you’ll transition seamlessly into your role, setting you up for success in the short and long term.
